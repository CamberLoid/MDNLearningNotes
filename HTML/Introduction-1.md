### References of special characters

They are `< > " ' &`  
Use below **Entity references** instead.

```
< 	&lt;
> 	&gt;
" 	&quot;
' 	&apos;
& 	&amp;
```

<p>&lt; &gt; &quot; &apos; &amp;</p>

### Single/Double Quotes

Refer to Python.  
Can use entity reference instead.
